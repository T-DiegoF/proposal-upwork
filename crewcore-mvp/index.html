Project control

Project, phase, assignment, and task management with simple access per role.

  • Assign crews by project and phase

  • Organize daily progress updates

Reports and evidence

Daily reports, internal chat, and media uploads all connected to the right job context.

Focused access for every role.

Admin, manager, worker, and client each get a different level of access without making the product feel complicated.

A

Admin

Full control over users, roles, projects, reports, and visibility settings.

M

Manager

Daily operational control for assignments, reports, chat, and field follow-up.

W

Worker

Simple action-based UI for attendance, tasks, messages, and media capture.

C

Client

Read-only access to approved progress information, reports, and shared files.
